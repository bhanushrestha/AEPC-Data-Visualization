Human and Animal Waste

Human and animal waste is collected in a concrete anaerobic digestion tank.

Anaerobic Digestion Tank

Waste is mixed with water and left to ferment.

Biogas for Cooking and Lighting

Biogas is produced as a byproduct of the fermentation and piped into the house when needed.

Benefits: Environment and Energy Security, Improved Saving and Extra Working Hours & Health

Producing biogas diminishes use of non-renewable energy. By switching to biogas people will not have to pay for firewood and kerosene and they can use the extra hours gained from not having to collect firewood elsewhere. Installing a biogas system in a house improves the health of a family, especially that of women and children
